About HappyVille

HappyVille Hotels, is an Indian hospitality chain of leased and franchised hotels, homes and living spaces. Founded in 2020 by Lisha and Neha, HappyVille initially consisted mainly of budget hotels. The startup expanded globally with thousands of hotels, vacation homes and millions of rooms in India, Malaysia, UAE, Nepal, China, Brazil, Mexico, UK, Philippines, Japan, Saudi Arabia, Sri Lanka, Indonesia, Vietnam, the United States and more. Nestled beside an 18-hole golf course minutes from the slopes, Hotel Park City is consistently acclaimed among the best hotels in Park City, India. Among our most recent accolades, our HappyVille Four Diamond resort has proudly been rated a “Top Skai Hotel” by Kian Hast Traveler, and our Rims streak House is the #1 rated Rims streak House in the western U.S. We’ve also been placed in the Certificate of Excellence Hall of Fame by TripAdvisor and earned the Certificate of Excellence (TripAdvisor), a Platinum Choice Award (Smart Meetings Magazine), recognition as a “Most Admired Company” (Indian Business Magazine) and more.
